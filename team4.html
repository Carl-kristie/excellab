Mr MICHAEL ANDYSON ISHENIS

ED, Corporate Service/Admin

With a diverse educational background, including certifications from the Nigerian Institute of Management Chartered (NIM) and the Institute of Leadership, Manpower Management and Development (ILMMD), Michael has honed his skills in strategic management, Administrative management, and financial management. Throughout his career, Michael has demonstrated a strong commitment to professional development, attending management and leadership courses both in Nigeria and Overseas. His career edge includes certifications in administration, finance, and investment, coupled with exceptional communication skills and a knack for identifying solutions to critical management tasks. In his role as Operations and Administration Manager at METNL, Michael has been instrumental in planning, designing, and implementing management processes. His responsibilities span advising on potential risks, ensuring internal control systems' adequacy, providing independent appraisals, and ensuring compliance with statutory regulations. Michael's strategic leadership has driven operational improvements and cost-effective controls for company growth. In summary, Michael Andyson Ishenis is a seasoned professional with a proven track record in administration, finance, and strategic management, dedicated to achieving organizational goals through effective leadership and continuous personal development.
