Mr Oghenedoro Tega Samson

CEO

Oghenedoro Tega Samson, is an experienced journalist with a willingness to embrace new ways of storytelling to achieve good results and a demonstrated history of complying with journalism ethics. Efficiently trained in investigative procedures and documentation, Tega holds a Bachelor of Arts, Theatre Arts degree from Imo State University, Owerri, Imo State. He is a crisis and reputation manager with a Master’s Degree in Linguistics and Communication from the University of Calabar. He is the Managing Editor of Secrets Reporters News platform, Managing Director of Excella B Topnotch Limited that's into mining of natural resources, with FG approved mining allocation. He's also the Managing Director of Excella U Casa Le Luxe Limited registered with REDAN, a real estate company providing state of the art buildings for its subscribers. As part of his duty towards being a patriot, he sits on the board of Network Against Corruption and Trafficking Foundation, Abuja, Nigeria as its Executive Director of Investigation, partnering with all Nigeria's anti-graft agencies towards the fight for a better Nigeria and Excella B Group. Tega is a member of the Nigerian Union of Journalist, (NUJ), Radio, Television, Theatre and Arts Workers Union, (RATTAWU) and former chairman of the Nigerian Television Authority, (NTA), Sapele, Delta State chapter. He is also a prominent member of the Online Publishers Association of Nigeria. Mr Tega is a tested political communicator and strategist that has midwifed people into government. He is well travelled within and outside Nigeria.
